Spanish Sausage Stew

Ingredients

  • 4 vegan sausages (e.g. Linda McCartney), defrosted
  • 1 large onion or leek
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 sweet potato or carrot
  • Handful cabbage, spinach, or kale
  • 1 tin chopped tomato
  • Veg stock or water
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried mixed herbs

Method

  1. Slice sausages into bite size pieces and lightly fry in oil until browning.
  2. Add onion or leek to pan with pinch of salt and cook until softened.
  3. Add red pepper, cook until onions or leeks have browned
  4. Add sweet potato or carrot, paprika and herbs. Stir and add tomatoes.
  5. Top up with water or veg stock, and allow to simmer until veg is softened.
  6. Taste and add salt, pepper and lemon juice to taste. Add greens and simmer for a further few minutes.
  7. Serve with bread.
