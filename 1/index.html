Tofu Tikka Masala

Ingredients

  • 1 block extra firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 peppers (red, yellow, orange)
  • 1 tomato, halved
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 heaped tsp tomato puree
  • 1 can coconut milk/cream
  • Curry powder (parts: 1 cumin, 1 ground coriander, 1 turmeric, 1 paprika, 0.25 hot chilli powder, 0.25 fennel, 0.25 cinnamon, 0.25 cardamom, 0.25 black pepper)
  • Garlic and ginger paste
  • Bunch fresh coriander

Method

  1. Coat tofu in oil and curry powder, place on baking tray.
  2. Hack up peppers and place on baking tray, with tofu and tomato. Allow to roast.
  3. When tofu is nearly golden and vegetables are nearly cooked, fry onion in oil for at least 10 minutes with salt.
  4. Add garlic and ginger paste, coriander stalks, and 2 heaped tablespoons of curry powder. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add onion-spice mixture to a blender along with roasted vegetables, tomato puree and stock to loosen. Blend until smooth
  6. Return to pan, simmer and add the cream of the coconut milk. Add the coconut water or more stock until smooth and creamy.
  7. Stir in tofu and fresh chopped coriander. Season with salt, a pinch of sugar and lemon/lime juice to taste. Serve with pilau rice and naan.
